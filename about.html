About NCI Gym

Our Story

Founded out of the necessity for fitness and wellness for our students, NCI Gym has grown into a community of dedicated fitness enthusiasts and professional trainers.

NCI Gym is the newest and most up to date club now open in the heart of the IFSC, based in the NCI and around the corner from Fresh, NCI Gym is a private health and fitness club like no other.

As part of NCI's campus, our gym complements the college's commitment to providing a complete educational experience. Whether you're a business student, or computing enthusiast, our facility is designed to support your physical well-being alongside your academic journey.
