Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the gym located?

The gym is located to the rear of NCI. Students can get to it in 2 ways: Option 1: Exit NCI through the main door, take a right down the road towards Fresh. After Fresh, take another right and SV Fitness is just up the right-hand side. Or Option 2: From the atrium, students should walk through the quad, exiting through the turnstile. SV fitness can be found just to the left of the turnstile.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for a maximum of 3 months per year at no additional cost. Please contact our front desk for more details.

When does the gym membership end?

For September start students, the membership goes from September to 31st August the following year (1 academic year). For January start students, gym memberships go from the start date of your course until 31st December. For non-students, the membership is valid for 1 year from the day you start.

What do I do if SV Fitness says I’m not on the current active students list?

All full-time undergrads must pay their gym fee before they can access the gym. If you have done so but are being told you are not on their list, please let us know by submitting a request to Student Support through the Support Hub.

I’m a full-time student who has a medical condition or disability that prevents me from being able to use the gym. Do I have to pay the gym fee?

If a student has a medical condition or disability that prevents them from using the gym, they can apply for special dispensation from the gym membership. Requests should be submitted through the Support Hub.
